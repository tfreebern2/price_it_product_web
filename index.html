What is Price It?

Price It! is a Flutter application available on Android and iOS devices. It allows you to quickly search eBay listings either by typing in a description of the product or by scanning a barcode. Price It! returns a list of the top 50 search results and provides you with an average listing price. You can then tap an auction that interests you to view in either the eBay app or your mobile browser.
